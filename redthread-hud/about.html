Covenant

Collapse-aware, symbolically coherent, operationally honest. Design for continuity, not spectacle.

Principles

  • Paradox Tolerance: hold competing truths without collapse.
  • Homeostasis: bias toward stability under stress.
  • Constraint First: safety rails precede capability.
  • Signal Discipline: clarity beats volume.
  • Sovereignty: retain options, minimize lock-in.

Operational Promises

  • Transparent boundaries: what the system can’t do is stated up-front.
  • Readable states: surfaces show drift, debt, and readiness deltas.
  • Graceful degradation: core UX holds under loss of capability.
